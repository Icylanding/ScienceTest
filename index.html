Biodiversity and Conservation

What is Biodiversity?

Biodiversity refers to the variety of life on Earth, from genes and species to ecosystems. It plays a crucial role in maintaining the balance of the natural world and supporting ecosystem services that humans rely on, such as clean air, water, and fertile soil.

Conservation Efforts

Conservation of biodiversity is essential to protect endangered species and preserve ecosystems. Conservation efforts include habitat protection, reforestation, and fighting climate change, all of which help maintain biodiversity and ensure the survival of many species, including pandas.
